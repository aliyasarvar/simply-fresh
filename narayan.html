How A Five-Star Chef’s Sacrifice Led Him to,
Serve Lakhs of Homeless With His Fresh Iraada

  • Share

Narayanan Krishnan, once an award-winning sous chef on the cusp of a prestigious international posting, experienced a life-altering moment during a visit to his hometown. He encountered an elderly homeless man so desperate for food that he was consuming his own waste. The sight shook him to his core and made him question the path he was on.

Driven by an unwavering sense of compassion, Narayanan
left behind his career in luxury kitchens to dedicate
himself to serving the homeless. He began by personally
preparing and distributing fresh, nutritious meals to those in
need, using his skills to bring dignity and hope to people
often overlooked by society.

What started as a small initiative has now transformed into a
movement of care and compassion. Today, countless lives
have been touched through his efforts, which extend
beyond food to include healthcare, clothing, and emotional
support. His journey is a powerful reminder of the change one
person can inspire with fresh iraade—a commitment to ,
making the world a better place through pure intentions and
selfless action.

With over 2 million meals served and his mission continuing to grow, Narayanan’s story is an inspiration to all, showing that a fresh perspective
and a determined heart can truly make a difference.
